BroCoder

Welcome to my YouTube channel BroCoder. In this Vlog, I share what I have learned while at MakerSquare as I go through Javascript toy problems, do live coding of applications, and use technologies like node.js, mongo.db, angular.js, react.js, mithrul.js, and many others.

Have You Heared The News

Have Your Heard The News is a news aggregator that retrieves nineteen news sources, sorts articles by good or bad news, and reads headlines to users via IBM's Watson. Have you heard the news stack consists of MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js.

Austin Art

An online gallery of public art in the Greater Austin Area that allows users to discover, rate, and comment on public artwork. Austin Art stack consists of MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js.

Makerspace

Room reservation app that shows real-time room availability, manages bookings, and gathers usage data for analytics. Makerspace stack consists of MongoDB, D3.js, Express.js, Socits.io React, Node.js. Please also watch app walkthrough.
